    Module 6
    When It Gets Hard
    Everyone hits obstacles. Here's how to push through.
    "I hate asking for money"
    Reframe it! You're offering an opportunity, not begging.
    Focus on the mission, not the money. Most people WANT to help!
    "People aren't responding"
    Follow up! People are busy. One message is NOT enough.
    Send 2-3 follow-ups spaced a week apart. Many donations come from follow-ups!
    "Someone said no"
    That's okay! Thank them for considering it. Don't take it personally.
    Move on to the next person. Every "no" gets you closer to a "yes"!
